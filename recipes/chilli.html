Chilli Mayo Instructions

Back to About Page

Description: This recipe will guide you through every step on how to create a chilli crisp mayo sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon spicy chili crisp, such as Lao Gan Ma®1 tablespoon spicy chili crisp, such as Lao Gan Ma®

Steps

  1. Place mayonnaise and chili crisp into the bowl of a mini food processor. Blend until smooth and evenly combined, scraping down the sides of the bowl. Alternately, you can stir the mayonnaise and chili crisp together in a bowl.
